About Us

The BRIDGE Initiative is focused on fostering collaboration and engagement across communities in the region. We strive to create opportunities that build connections and empower individuals.

Our Mission

The goal of our initiative is to be a point of connection and a mode of outreach for Bard students to engage civically with our broader Hudson Valley community. We look to do this through hosting speaking events, facilitating community conversations, and providing transport to local opportunities for civic engagement.
